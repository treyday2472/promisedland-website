What a Vacant House Really Costs You Every Month

Most owners underestimate how expensive an empty property is to hold.

💸

Property Taxes Keep Running

Dallas County property taxes don't stop just because nobody's living there. Depending on the value, that's hundreds to thousands per month in effective daily costs.

🛡️

Vacancy Insurance Is Expensive

Standard homeowner insurance often voids coverage after 30–60 days of vacancy. Specialized vacant property insurance costs significantly more — and your lender may require it.

🏚️

Accelerated Deterioration

Vacant houses deteriorate faster than occupied ones. Small leaks become major water damage. HVAC issues go unnoticed. A foundation crack spreads. Every month it sits, the repair bill grows.

🚨

Vandalism and Squatters

Vacant homes attract break-ins, copper theft, and in some cases squatters who can be legally difficult to remove. One incident can cause tens of thousands in damage.

Vacant Property Questions — Answered

Do I need to clean out the property before selling?
No. We buy vacant properties as-is — whatever is inside stays or gets removed by us after closing. You take anything you want to keep and leave the rest.
What if the property has squatters or was broken into?
We deal with this more often than you'd think. We'll factor the situation into the offer and handle the property as-is. In some cases we may need a clear title before closing, which the title company helps coordinate.
I live out of state — can I still sell?
Absolutely. We handle out-of-state sellers regularly. The entire process can be done remotely — electronic signing, remote notarization, and proceeds wired directly to your bank account.
What if there are back taxes owed on the property?
Back taxes are paid from the sale proceeds at closing. You don't need to resolve them before selling — they come out of what you receive at settlement.
The property hasn't had utilities on in years. Can you still make an offer?
Yes. We make offers on vacant properties all the time without utilities being active. We'll do a walk-through inspection and factor the condition into our offer accordingly.
