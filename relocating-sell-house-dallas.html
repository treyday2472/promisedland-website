The Problem With Listing Your House When You're Moving

A traditional listing while relocating creates a logistical nightmare.

✈️

You're Not Here to Manage It

Once you've moved, you're managing the listing remotely. Scheduling showings, approving repairs, and responding to offers while you're in another city is stressful and slow.

💸

Two Sets of Housing Costs

Every month the Dallas house sits unsold, you're paying a mortgage (or rent) in your new city AND carrying costs on the old house. That's two mortgages, two insurance policies, two utility bills.

⏱️

Dallas Listings Average 45–90+ Days

Even in a good market, a traditional sale takes time — inspections, appraisals, mortgage approvals, negotiations. That's months of your life tied up in a transaction you want finished.

🔧

Buyers Always Want Repairs

After the inspection, buyers submit a repair request. Now you're hiring contractors in a city you no longer live in, coordinating work remotely, and hoping it gets done right.

Relocating and Selling — Questions Answered

Can I sell if I've already moved out and the house is empty?
Yes — and this is actually ideal for a cash sale. Vacant properties are easier to close quickly. We can do a walkthrough while you're not there, and closing can be handled remotely or with a power of attorney if needed.
Can I close remotely if I'm already in my new city?
Absolutely. We facilitate remote closings regularly. Documents can be signed electronically or through a mobile notary in your new city, and proceeds are wired directly to your bank account.
What if I need the equity from this house to buy in my new city?
That's the most common reason sellers call us. We can work with you on a close date that lines up with your purchase in the new city, so you have cash in hand when you need it.
What if my employer is paying for my relocation — does that change anything?
Some employers have relocation programs that work directly with investors. We can work with corporate relo programs as well as direct sellers. Let us know and we'll coordinate accordingly.
Is your offer lower because I'm in a hurry?
We make the same fair offer regardless of your urgency. Our offer is based on the property's value and condition — not how motivated you are to sell. We don't lowball sellers in tough situations.
