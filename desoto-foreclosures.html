DeSoto Foreclosure — Common Questions

What does a trustee sale notice mean in DeSoto?
It means a lender has filed public notice that they intend to foreclose on a property in Dallas County, TX. In Texas, the sale is scheduled for the first Tuesday of the following month after a 21-day posting period. The homeowner still has time to act before the auction.
Can I sell my DeSoto house if it is in foreclosure?
Yes. As long as the foreclosure sale has not happened yet, you can sell your home, pay off the lender, and potentially walk away with remaining equity. Cash buyers like Promised Land Property Solutions can close in as few as 7 days.
