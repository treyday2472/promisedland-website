Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about selling your house to us.

How fast can you close?
We can close in as few as 7 days. If you need more time, we can work with your schedule — 30, 60, or 90 days. You pick the date.
Do I need to make repairs before selling?
No. We buy houses as-is, in any condition. Don't spend a dollar on repairs, cleaning, or staging. We handle everything after closing.
Are there any fees or commissions?
Zero. No realtor fees, no closing costs, no hidden charges. The price we agree on is the amount you receive at closing.
Can you help if I'm already in foreclosure?
Yes. We specialize in helping homeowners facing foreclosure. We can often move fast enough to stop the foreclosure sale and protect your credit. Call us immediately — time matters.
What types of properties do you buy?
Single-family homes, duplexes, town homes, inherited properties, vacant land, and more — anywhere in the Dallas-Fort Worth metro area.
Is there any obligation if I get an offer?
Absolutely not. Our offer is 100% free with no obligation. You can decline the offer with zero pressure.
